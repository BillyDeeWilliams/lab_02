What is 'danna'?

Dāna (दान) is a Sanskrit and Pali word that connotes the virtue of generosity, charity or giving of alms in Indian philosophies. ... In Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism and Sikhism, dāna is the practice of cultivating generosity.
